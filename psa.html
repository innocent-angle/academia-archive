Inevitably someone will approach you with one or both of these offers, and I want you to know in advance what they gain from it and why it probably isn't in your best interest. Newer players will be affected by these the most.

 

Loss Selling

 

Attacking someone and losing intentionally is worth a certain amount of money, usually 300K-400K each. Even at these prices I don't consider it worth it, unless you just do it a couple times for a Xanax to attack with. But it gets worse, if you're a new player people are going to spam mail you offering you as low as 50k-100k. If you REALLY want to sell losses, find reputable buyers on the attacking forum and accept no less than 300k each.

 

Faction Poachers

 

It's not uncommon for other factions to try to "poach" members and get them to switch. Sometimes this is genuine interest, but 90% of the time there is a reason they can't get anyone to join and have resorted to poaching. This is not me trying to get you to stay, this is me trying to keep you informed that these people are not genuinely interested in you. If you want to go to a warring faction, that's totally fine, but please don't join one of these trash cache farms that will take advantage of you.
