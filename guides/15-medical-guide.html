Blood Types & Medical Cooldown

 

For new players, med kits and morphine are usually fine, but at some point in your Torn journey you will need to carefully manage your medical cooldown and use proper efficient items. Once your medical cooldown is maxed there aren't any other cheap/easy ways out of the hospital. 

 

  Hospital Reduction Life Granted
Small First Aid Kit 20 minutes 5%
First Aid Kit 40 minutes 10%
Morphine 70 minutes 15%
Blood Bags 120 minutes 30% life

 

Blood Bags

 

Blood bags are the most efficient medical item, and it's important that you complete BIO2127 early on to unlock them. Once this course is finished, you can learn what blood type you are by filling an empty blood bag, which we have plenty of in the armory. It's also much appreciated if you can fill blood bags occasionally since they are quite expensive to supply for 100 people. There are a few merits for filling blood bags as well, so might as well help us out.

 

 

Blood bags will take 120-180 minutes off your hospital time and restore 30% of your total Life and add 30 minutes to your medical cooldown. It is important not to just always take blood bags. Try to use the best item to minimize your medical cooldown. 

 

Using the wrong blood type will send you to the hospital for up to ~90 minutes. This can actually be very useful, for example a common strategy while trading or selling on the item market is to list everything and then quickly hospitalize yourself, then use a ghost trade to store your money before coming out of the hospital.

 

Being AB+ is a bit unlucky, since you can't intentionally use any wrong blood bag to self hospitalize. You can, however, use Ipecac Syrup which has basically the same effect.
