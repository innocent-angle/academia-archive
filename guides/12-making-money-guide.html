Making Money (Work in progress)

 

Flying (Recommended)

 

First major income source for most people. Requires level 15. We have an entire guide dedicated to it above.

 

Buying city items (Recommended)

 

There are NPC shops in the city you can buy items from, such as Bits N' Bobs. They have a limit (usually 100 per day) which makes them profitable to buy and resell on the item market. Make sure to check market prices yourself, but bottles of beer and plushies are basically always profitable.

 

Selling losses (not recommended)

 

Chances are as a new player someone will approach you offering to pay you some tiny amount of money to lose to them. This is not worth it and should be avoided. However, If you're absolutely certain you want to sell losses, sell to players in the attacking forum and accept no less than 300k per loss.
