There are two very popular third party tools / apps that you might be interested in using. All of the tools below are safe, legal, and maintained by trusted Torn community members. I personally have used TornTools for years and can't even imagine playing the game without it at this point.

 

You must provide an API key (limited, usually) when using most of these tools.

It is the legal way apps and scripts can get information from the game.

 

TornTools - Browser Extension

 

Bunch of tools and quality of life improvements to the desktop / browser version of the game. Cannot be used on mobile as far as I know.
