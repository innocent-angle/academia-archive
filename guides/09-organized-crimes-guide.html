Organized Crimes Guide

 

Organized crimes are this faction’s bread and butter. You will team up with other faction members, plan and commit various crimes that deposit rewards into the faction vault. These rewards are then split between participating members, with a small cut going to the faction funds to pay for supplies.

 

 

Short version:

 

  1. Go to the crimes tab on the faction page
  2. Join the highest level team you have ~70 CSR or in, up to level 4.
  3. Hover over the role name. If an item is required, you must buy it from the item market.
  4. Be in Torn (not flying/hosp/jail) when the timer ends, and online within the previous 24 hours of the timer ending.
  5. You should get a payout within 24 hours if the crime was a success.

 

If you don't have ~70 CSR in any team, please make sure to spend all your booster cooldown on beers in the armory and do nerve crimes so you can start doing them ASAP. It should only take about a week before you can start doing level 1s.

 

If you get a 0% cut, it's because someone on your team caused an issue (delay, didn't buy required item, etc.). You'll still get paid, we just have to do it manually so it might take a bit longer.

 

Planning

 

As soon as the first member joins, regardless of role, a countdown begins. Every role requires 24 hours of planning before the crime can be initiated, and they will be planned back to back until all roles are finished. This is passive and you don't have to be online. The final countdown shown above the team is when the crime will be automatically initiated, assuming nobody causes a delay.

 

You must have been online within the previous 24 hours, and healthy in torn (not flying, hosped, or jailed) once the timer ends, or the crime will be delayed. If you delay a crime for any significant amount of time without notice, your payout will be divided among the other members of your team as compensation to them.

 

Planning format is Day:Hour:Minute:Second
