Education Guide

 

Education is somewhat controversial in Torn, hardly anyone can agree on what order to take them in. Here I will outline some that I think everyone should consider first, and then I'll give you some to think about depending on your goals. Eventually you should take them all.

 

Note: The education merits are highly recommended, check our merit guide as well!

 

Good general starting order for everyone:

 

  1. Biology > Introduction to Biochemistry
  2. Biology > Intravenous Therapy (unlocks blood bags)
  3. Sports Science > Introduction to Sports Science
  4. Sports Science > All the ones that give gym gains
  5. Combat Training > Everything & Bachelor (unlocks weapon XP)
  6. Biology > Intermediate Biochemistry (for increased medical efficiency)
  7. Biology > Advanced Biochemistry (for increased medical efficiency)
  8. Various Categories > Passive % Battle Stat Boost

 

Intravenous Therapy is widely regarded as the most important education course. It gives you access to the most efficient medical items in the game, and allows you to hospitalize yourself by using the wrong blood type. It is also greatly appreciated if you fill empty blood bags in the armory to help cut costs.

 

At some point you should really create your own plan. Ask yourself the following questions:

 

Do you want to run a company some day?

 

If so, I recommend doing some research into what it takes to lead a company. It isn't as hard as people make it out to be, but it does require you to be active almost every single day and put some effort into recruiting. If you are okay with all of this, start doing all of the business education right away, they are one of the best investments in the game. 

 

Do you want a boost to all future education course work stat gains?

 

Completing the general education bachelor gives you a permanent 10% boost to work stat gains from education. This might be worth it, but that can only be decided by you, as it will take you over a month to complete them which could also be spent on anything else.

 

Are you getting close to bootlegging?

 

There is a required education in the computer science category for setting up an online store.

 

Are you getting close to the hacking crimes?

 

There is a required education in the computer science category that is required for hacking crimes.

 

(Obsolete) Are you getting close to the driving crimes? 

Note: This was used for an old crime, but a future crime will likely require it again.

 

There is a required education in the general education category that is required for driving crimes.

 

Do you want to bust/bail people out of jail?

 

I highly recommend completing all of the law crimes as they give a huge buff to these actions.

 

Want better / more frequent city finds?

 

Consider browsing the history category.

 

Want to be a trader / create item sets?

 

The History bachelor is required to turn item sets into points for profit.

 

Want better ammo efficiency?

 

Consider the mathematics bachelor for 20% more ammo per magazine(?)
