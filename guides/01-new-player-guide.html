New Player Guide

 

This is a super condensed version of all the other guides, intended for people who don't want to do a bunch of reading. You are welcome and encouraged to just do whatever you want, this early in the game you can't really make any "wrong" decisions as long as you are spending your energy and nerve on something. Come back and read specific guides if/when you need more help. Feel free to ask for help in chat as well, every question is valid.

 

  • Get a job
  • Start an education
  • Train in the gym every day
  • Spend your nerve on crimes, do them in order
  • Once your recruit status ends, grab some gear out of the armory
  • Once your recruit status ends, read our OC guide and join a team
  • Once you have ~500-1000 total stats start attacking previous mission targets or inactives to level up
