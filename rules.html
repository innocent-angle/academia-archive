Conduct:

 

In general, you're expected to be respectful to each other and maintain our neutral standing in Torn.

 

  1. No scamming, stealing, begging, including outside of the faction
  2. No drama, no politics, be respectful to your faction mates
  3. No attacks or bounties on your faction mates unless permitted or it benefits them
  4. You must, if able, participate in at least one organized crime per week, without delays
  5. You must not antagonize people or drag us into pointless conflicts
  6. No spam, large copy pastes, advertising, or otherwise disruptive faction chat

 

If you delay an OC for any significant amount of time, you will not get a cut of the rewards.

 

* Warning for muggers / buymuggers: We have no issues with mugging as a playstyle, but you WILL be on your own when it comes to the enemies you pick up from doing so. Do not go this route until you have high stats or are ready to sit in the hospital 24/7 when someone inevitably gets upset with you.

 

Armory:

 

  1. Do not deposit low tier (worse than Enfields/combat armor) equipment in the armory
  2. You must return RW weapons (yellow, orange, red) if you aren't actively using them
  3. If you use Xanax from the armory, it must be used for whatever event it was unlocked
